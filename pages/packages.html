Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our kitesurfing packages

Dakhla offers excellent kitesurfing conditions almost year-round. However, the prime seasons are from April to September when the wind is most consistent, averaging 15-25 knots. The water temperature ranges from 18-22°C, making a light wetsuit comfortable for most visitors.

No prior experience is necessary for our beginner packages. We start with the fundamentals and progress at your pace. Basic swimming skills are required for safety reasons. Our experienced instructors will guide you through every step of the learning process.

All our packages include complete equipment: kites, boards, harnesses, wetsuits, and safety gear. We use top-quality gear from leading brands, regularly maintained and replaced. We have equipment suitable for all sizes and skill levels.
