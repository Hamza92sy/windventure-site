Dakhla Excursions

Explore the natural wonders surrounding Dakhla

Discover Dakhla's Hidden Gems

While Dakhla is renowned for its world-class kitesurfing conditions, the region also boasts breathtaking natural landscapes that deserve exploration. Our excursions take you beyond the water to experience the unique beauty of this remote corner of Morocco.

From pristine white sand dunes to desert oases, these guided adventures complement your kitesurfing experience and provide unforgettable memories of this extraordinary destination.

Our Featured Excursions

Unique experiences in and around Dakhla

La Dune Blanche

Half Day Moderate Scenic

Rising majestically from the waters of the Dakhla lagoon, La Dune Blanche (The White Dune) is one of the most iconic natural landmarks in the region. This immense sand dune appears to float in the middle of the azure waters, creating a surreal and breathtaking landscape.

Our excursion takes you by boat to this natural wonder where you can climb to the summit for panoramic views of the lagoon and surrounding desert. The constantly shifting sands and changing light throughout the day make each visit unique and perfect for photography enthusiasts.

Experience Includes:

  • Boat transportation to and from the dune
  • Guided climb to the summit
  • Time for swimming and photography
  • Refreshments and snacks

Plage Portorico

Full Day 4x4 Adventure Beach

Located on the ocean side of the Dakhla peninsula, Plage Portorico is a pristine stretch of golden sand where the desert meets the Atlantic Ocean. This untouched beach offers a stark contrast to the calm waters of the lagoon, with impressive waves crashing against the shore and creating a dramatic seascape.

Our 4x4 journey takes you across rugged desert terrain to reach this remote beach paradise. Upon arrival, you'll have time to walk along the shore, search for unique seashells, or simply relax and soak in the wild beauty of the Atlantic coast.

Experience Includes:

  • Round-trip 4x4 transportation
  • Guided exploration of the beach
  • Picnic lunch with local delicacies
  • Sunset viewing (for afternoon excursions)

Desert Adventure

Full Day 4x4 Off-Road Cultural

Venture deep into the Sahara Desert surrounding Dakhla on this immersive adventure. The landscape transforms as you travel inland, revealing vast sand dunes, rocky plateaus, and occasional acacia trees that dot the otherwise barren terrain.

This excursion offers an opportunity to experience traditional nomadic culture, with a visit to a Sahrawi camp where you can learn about desert life, enjoy traditional mint tea, and witness the remarkable hospitality of the desert people. The journey also includes stops at desert oases and unique geological formations.

Experience Includes:

  • Full-day 4x4 exploration with experienced guides
  • Visit to a Sahrawi nomadic camp
  • Traditional Moroccan lunch
  • Tea ceremony and cultural demonstration
  • Desert wildlife spotting opportunities

Dragon Island

Half Day Boat Trip Wildlife

Situated in the calm waters of Dakhla Bay, Dragon Island is a natural sanctuary for a variety of bird species and marine life. Named for its distinctive shape that resembles a sleeping dragon from above, this uninhabited island offers a peaceful retreat from the mainland.

Our boat excursion takes you around and onto the island, where you can observe flamingos, herons, and other migratory birds in their natural habitat. The surrounding waters are crystal clear, providing excellent opportunities for swimming and snorkeling to discover the underwater life of the bay.

Experience Includes:

  • Guided boat tour around the island
  • Bird watching with knowledgeable guides
  • Swimming and snorkeling opportunity (equipment provided)
  • Fresh seafood lunch prepared on board
