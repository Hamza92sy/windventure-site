Why Rent With Us?

Our rental fleet features the latest models of kitesurfing equipment, meticulously maintained and regularly replaced to ensure optimal performance and safety.

Whether you're an experienced rider looking to travel light or a beginner wanting to try different gear, our rental options provide flexibility and convenience for your Dakhla adventure.

All rentals include basic instruction on setup and safety features if needed. Our team can also recommend the most suitable equipment based on your skill level and the day's conditions.

Rental Information

  • Rental Hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM, daily
  • Pickup Location: Windventure Center, Dakhla Bay
  • Advance Booking: Recommended, especially during peak season
  • Requirements: Valid ID and credit card for deposit
  • Wind Forecast: Available daily at our center
